Moderator Panel Guide

Kick: Temporarily remove a user from the session.

Mute: Prevent a user from sending messages.

Message: Send a message to Staff HQ group chat.

Role Dropdown: Moderators can assign roles VIP 10 → VIP 1 → Member only. Cannot touch Admin, Mods, or Security Staff.

Notifications: Moderators can send announcements to all users, but not to specific individuals.
